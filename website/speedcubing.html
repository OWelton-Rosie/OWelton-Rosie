What is speedcubing?

Speedcubing (also known as speedsolving or cubing), is the competitive solving of Rubik's Cube-like puzzles. Global speedcubing is sanctioned by the World Cube Association (WCA).

My speedcubing journey

I have been speedcubing since December 2023, and have conpeted in 10 WCA-recognised competitions. I am currently a member of the Speedcubing New Zealand Promotional Team and a member of the Speedcubing New Zealand Stream Team.

I use the 4 Look Last Layer method to solve the Rubik's Cube in under 20 seconds on average. I am currently ranked 72nd in New Zealand for Pyraminx average, with a result of 5.67 seconds.

Some of my notable speedcubing achievements

  • Co-organiser of Just Solve in Johnsonville 2025
  • In March 2025, I set a Pyraminx average of 5.76 seconds, placing me 72nd in New Zealand
