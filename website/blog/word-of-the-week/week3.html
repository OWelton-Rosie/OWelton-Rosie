Word of the week - Tuesday, 19th August 2025

Formulaic

This week's word is formulaic.

Definition

Consisting of or resembling a formula.

Usage/examples

Many of the songs on the radio are formulaic, following the same structure and themes.

The movie was criticized for its formulaic plot, which offered nothing new or exciting
