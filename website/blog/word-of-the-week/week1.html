Word of the week - Monday, 4th August 2025

Posted By Oscar on 4th August 2025

Notwithstanding

This week's word is notwithstanding.

Definition

In spite of.

Usage/examples

Notwithstanding recent diversity drives, our workplace is dominated by people from the same background.

Notwithstanding the bad weather, we had a picnic at the beach.

About this series

"Word of the week" is a series that I'll be (trying to!) post semi-regularly. I'll choose words that I find interesting or have recently come across.
