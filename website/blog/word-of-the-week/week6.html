Word of the week - Monday, 8th September 2025

Posted By Oscar on 8th September 2025

Homogeneous

This week's word is homogeneous.

Definition

All the same; alike.

Examples

The class was homogeneous, with nearly all the students sharing similar backgrounds and interests.

The mixture looked homogeneous, so it was impossible to tell the individual ingredients apart.
